Mobile website instructions:

NB! In order to view if any 'Airline Employee Rate' is available, you have to switch the layout from 'Mobile view' to normal 'Desktop view'.

If you're forced into mobile view, then touch the 'View Full Website' on the bottom of the homepage.

1: 'Find a Hotel'

2: Mark the 'Airline Employee' field

If you don't see the 'Airline Employee' field, it means there are no available rooms on the chosen dates.
