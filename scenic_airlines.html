Good to Know
 
Best time to travel
 
All of our flights are subject to weather, seat and weight availability. It helps to be flexible with departure times and tours. May – September is our busy season. Weekends tend to be slower and early morning departures give you the most options.
 
Dress code
 
Our dress code is casual and comfortable. Wear “closed-toe” shoes and dress for an outdoors experience. Jeans and tennis shoes okay, but all has to be neat and clean. No torn clothing, offensive lettering, short skirts and shorts, bare midriffs. During the winter months bring a warm sweater or/and jacket.
 
Ticketing/Listing
 
Travel is ticketless. 
 
Call 24-48 hours prior to list and to inquire about availability.
 
US toll free: 888-635-7272. Worldwide: +1 702-638-3300. Office hours are 5AM – 11PM.
 
We will need actual body weights to list you!
 
ID requirement
 
Government issued ID plus your valid airline ID. 
 
Traveling without the employee and no company ID? Show us one of the following: Copy of your domestic or international non revenue/standby itinerary, copy of your MYID page showing you as an eligible traveler  
 
Form of payment
 
You will pay once your seats have cleared by the ticket counter staff. All major credit cards are accepted. Cash US$ only.
 
 
 
Departures from Las Vegas, Nevada (LAS)
